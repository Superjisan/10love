For every hateful action that happens in Trump's name, let's send 10 messages of love, hope, inspiration, and solidarity to those affected.

Mission

Since the election of Donald Trump, hateful and bigoted acts have proliferated throughout the United States, especially in our K-12 schools.

The mission of this website is to send 10 messages for every single hateful thing that has happened to those students involved.

Let's empower the next generation that the United States stands for acceptance, love, kindness, and resilience in the face of hatred and bigotry.
